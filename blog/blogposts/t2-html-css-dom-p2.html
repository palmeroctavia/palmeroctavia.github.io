What is grid based design?

Grid based design is when the webpage is seperated into rows and columns (usually 12 columns of equal size). This allows front-end developers to easily lay out content in a clean and visually-appealing way.

Whats all the hype about responsive webpages?

The hype is that responsiveness makes your website work (in terms of design) on multiple devices (e.g.phone/tablet/large PC screen/laptop). Responsiveness ensures that structural elements such as columns change in size depending on the resolution of device screens e.g. shrinking the webpage from a PC screen to fit a smartphone screen.

What is semantic structure?

Semantic structure is when elements such as headings or paragraphs are used to provide additional structure to content. This is because people tend to prefer using different sizing/weight of font to provide more/less emphasis or importance to content. For example, in the heirarchy of text h1 tends to be larger and more important than body text found in a paragraph.
